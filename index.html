CowinHood

Not just the fastest in finding slots, but the fastest in delivering them to YOU.

CowinHood doesn't just send 1 alert per minute for open sessions. It updates telegram messages with real time slot changes (multiple times per second). The benefits are two-fold: avoid redundant alerts for districts with abundant availability of slots, and for real time tracking of slot changes for districts with scarce availability of slots.

CowinHood is built with love using asynchronous IO, telegram token pooling, session history tracking, aggregation and intelligent inference of relevant changes. The fact that it is fastest at polling cowin servers (capable 1000s of times per second, although not configured), is built to scale with fine grained locks and a highly parallelizable shared-nothing actor programming model, aren't even it's biggest highlights. Everything is configurable and the config and metrics are open for the world to see.

Full tracking history

CowinHood doesn't just poll and forward slots to telegram. It maintains detailed tracking history of each and every slot availability change to each and every session, and effectively micro-batching them based on configured intervals per channel, based on that city's frequency of new slots. It can switch between a detailed view showing all updates and a summary view showing statistically significant updates (a inverse exponential distribution of the raw data). It also takes care of common anomalies in slot data.
